About Wise Adviser

Abstract

“Wise Advisor” is a Web-based recommender system for the novice researchers to facilitate them in selecting the potential research topics and related influential researchers.

  • Ranking the influential researchers through a comprehensive and sound researcher’s ranking methodology.
  • List of the potential research areas, extracted from publications of the influential researchers
  • List of top universities
  • Provision of the researcher’s research interests and affiliation to facilitate the research collaborations.
